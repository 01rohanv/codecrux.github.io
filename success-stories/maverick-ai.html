We Codecrux team is happy and excited to share the success story of our client Maverick.ai ,which had been acquired by Outboundwork.

On 18th December 2018,Outbound Works ,Inc..a San Francisco,CA-based automated sales development platform provider,acquired Maverick.ai,a provider of AI enabled B2B lead generation platform.

“I’m truly excited to combine forces with outbound works ,a company i have been following since starting Maverick.ai and widely considered the leader in pushing the sales development industry forward through the use of automation and machine learning”
- Yaron Oren ,founder & CEO of Maverick.ai
Website:http://maverick.ai
Youtube: https://youtu.be/NXD_ktO-vxM

The amount of the deal between the two companies was not disclosed.This is outbound works second sales development technology acquisition this year after the company acquired HEXA.ai following this year in the month of february.

Outbound Works will retain the Maverick.ai team, along with its technology and data assets.Maverick.ai Founder and CEO Yaron Oren spent the last 20 years in the tech industry of which 10 years he was a sale & marketing leader.He build 4 successful business along the way and hold and Engineering degree from Cornell and MBA from Kellogg school of management.

Bryan Franklin CEO and Ben Sardella ,CRO of Outbound Works; combine man and technology to deliver qualified sales appointment for fast growing companies in tech world.
