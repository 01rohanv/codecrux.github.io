Tips to become more productive

Tips to become more productive

  1. Speed of implimentation - Most of successful persons has very fast rate of absorption informations and implementations.
  2. Value of your time - You should be knowing value of your time, so before doing any work or time pass just think about your value of time.
  3. Use your time wisely - you have ability to accomplish any thing, any thing in this world.. if you use your time wisely
  4. Availability of taking decision in uncertainty - You need to practice for this a lot.
  5. Stay Focused - Set priorities so you can stay focused
  6. Be honest and disciplined
  7. Set deadlines - set work structure on basis of its priority and work accordingly
  8. Keep distractions away while at work - Social media, personal long talks and other distractions shall stay away when you are at work
  9. Limit work from home
